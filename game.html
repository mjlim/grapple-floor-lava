controls: w/s extend/retract rope. a/d to move left/right. space to detach hook. left click to fire hook. hook is currently affected by gravity. may or may not keep this behaviour. right click to fire yank hook that pulls you towards it quickly

or, if you have a 360 pad attached, left stick to aim hook & move left/right and up/down to extend/retract rope; LT for swinging hook and RT for yank hook. gamepad support currently only tested in chrome
